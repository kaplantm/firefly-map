Map of the 'Verse
This map of the Firefly universe was created by Toni Kaplan for NMD 345 at UMaine. This interactive map displays the major suns, planets and moons from the universe of the Firefly television show. The Firefly and Serenity Database was used as a reference for this project, as was Stemkoski's Three.js tutorial archive.
